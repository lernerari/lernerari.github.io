Hi! I am a PhD student in Operations Research at the Yale School of Management. I am interested in algorithm & mechanism design for social good and non-profit applications. Prior to graduate school, I spent two years as an economic consultant at Analysis Group where I focused on energy, climate & natural resources and health economics & outcomes research. I received a B.S. in Mathematics from Tufts University.

In my free time, I enjoy charcoal drawing, reading literary fiction and essays (a list of favorites), watching old Soviet films, and feeling the unnecessary stress of supporting both Chelsea FC and my hometown Boston Celtics.
